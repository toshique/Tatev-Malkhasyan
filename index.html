What about Dolmaing?

Why do we like dolma?

  1. In every armenian family Dolma is cooking not too often, so we miss this meal.
  2. There are different types of Dolmas, so you can find the one that you would like the most.
    • Vine leaves
    • Cabbage
    • Mixed(tomatoes, eggplant, pepper)
  3. Because your mother is the best cooker of Dolma.

Let's see what make Dolma so tasty.

  1. Groung beef
  2. Rice
  3. Tomatoes
  4. Butter
  5. Basil
  6. Coriander
  7. Rosemary
  8. Cumin
  9. Parsley
  10. Pepper(black, red)
  11. Salt
  12. Vine leaves(or cabbage, eggplants, peppers)

But all we know that the cooking proccess is the most difficult and vital.

  1. Mix ground beef, rice, chopped coriander, cumin, rosemary, parsley.
  2. Add salt, peppers and start to mix well all the ingridients by hand.
  3. Bewet leaves of vine with hot water.
  4. Finally, start to roll meat in vine leaves.
  5. Put all Dolmas in a deep caldron.
  6. Now it is time for making the souse for our Dolma.
    • Chop tomatoes untill it has no big parts.
    • Put some butter in a pan and add chopped tomatoes.
    • Add salt and pepper. So, the souse is ready.
  7. Pour the sous and water(untill it covers Dolmas) on the Dolmas.
  8. Close the caldron.
  9. Add put on the low heat.
  10. It took approximately an hour for being ready.

Yeess, Dolmas are ready and it's time for Dolmaing. Put some(20:D) Dolmas on the plate, add macoun with garlic. Finally, you can eat them.
